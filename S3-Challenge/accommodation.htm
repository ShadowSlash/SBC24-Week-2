Accommodation on the Isle of Wight

A Place to Stay for Every Traveler

The Isle of Wight offers a diverse range of accommodation options to suit every taste and budget. Whether you’re looking for luxury hotels, cozy bed and breakfasts, or family-friendly holiday parks, you’ll find the perfect place to stay on this charming island.

Hotels on the Isle of Wight

For those seeking a touch of luxury, the Isle of Wight boasts several high-end hotels and resorts.

  • Lakeside Park Hotel & Spa: This 4-star hotel offers indulgent spa facilities, picturesque views, and extensive leisure amenities including a swimming pool, hot tub, sauna, and steam room. Guests can enjoy free breakfast, Wi-Fi, and parking.
  • Auckland House Hotel: Located in Shanklin, this 5-star accommodation features a hot tub and sun terrace. Guests can savor a delicious à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

These hotels provide a perfect blend of comfort and elegance, ensuring a memorable stay on the island.

Airbnb on the Isle of Wight

For a more intimate and homely experience, the Isle of Wight has numerous Airbnb options scattered across the island. These accommodations offer a warm welcome and a personal touch, often with home-cooked breakfasts and local insights from the hosts.

  • The Caledon Guest House: Located in Cowes, this B&B is known for its friendly atmosphere and delicious breakfasts made with locally sourced ingredients.
  • The Clifton: Situated in Shanklin, this award-winning guest house offers stunning sea views and a peaceful garden, perfect for a relaxing stay.

Airbnb options on the Isle of Wight provide a unique opportunity to experience local hospitality and charm.

Camping on the Isle of Wight

For those who love the great outdoors, camping on the Isle of Wight is an excellent choice. The island offers a variety of camping options, from traditional campsites to luxurious glamping experiences.

  • Whitecliff Bay Holiday Park: This park offers a range of accommodation options from caravans to luxury lodges. Facilities include indoor and outdoor pools, a private beach, and various entertainment options.
  • The Garlic Farm Yurts: Enjoy a glamping experience in luxurious yurts set on a working garlic farm, complete with farm tours and tastings.

Camping on the Isle of Wight allows visitors to immerse themselves in the island’s natural beauty and enjoy a truly unique stay.

Booking Your Stay

The Isle of Wight is a popular destination, especially during the summer months and festival seasons. It’s advisable to book your accommodation in advance to secure the best options. Many properties offer online booking, making it easy to plan your stay from the comfort of your home.
